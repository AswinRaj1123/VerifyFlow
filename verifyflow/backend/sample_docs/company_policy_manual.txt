# Company Policy Manual

## Section 1: Data Privacy and Security

### 1.1 Data Retention Policy
The company retains all customer data for a period of 7 years following the end of the business relationship. This retention period complies with GDPR requirements and industry best practices.

All customer data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption and in transit using TLS 1.3. Access to customer data is restricted to authorized personnel only and is logged for audit purposes.

### 1.2 Data Classification
Data is classified into three categories:
- **Public**: Information that can be freely shared
- **Internal**: Information for internal use only
- **Confidential**: Sensitive information requiring special protection

### 1.3 Security Incident Response
In the event of a security incident, the following steps must be taken:
1. Immediately contain the incident
2. Notify the Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)
3. Document all actions taken
4. Conduct a post-incident review within 48 hours

## Section 2: Compliance Requirements

### 2.1 Regulatory Compliance
The company maintains compliance with the following regulations:
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) where applicable
- Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS)

### 2.2 Audit Schedule
Internal audits are conducted quarterly by the Internal Audit team. External audits are performed annually by certified third-party auditors.

All audit findings must be addressed within 30 days of report issuance. Critical findings require immediate remediation within 7 business days.

## Section 3: Access Control

### 3.1 User Access Management
User access is granted based on the principle of least privilege. Access requests must be approved by the data owner and the employee's manager.

Access is reviewed quarterly and revoked immediately upon termination of employment or change of role.

### 3.2 Multi-Factor Authentication
Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required for:
- All remote access
- Access to production systems
- Administrative accounts
- Access to customer data

## Section 4: Business Continuity

### 4.1 Backup Policy
Data backups are performed:
- Daily incremental backups
- Weekly full backups
- Monthly backups retained for 12 months

Backup restoration is tested quarterly to ensure data recoverability.

### 4.2 Disaster Recovery
The company maintains a disaster recovery plan with a Recovery Time Objective (RTO) of 4 hours and Recovery Point Objective (RPO) of 1 hour for critical systems.

Disaster recovery tests are conducted semi-annually.
