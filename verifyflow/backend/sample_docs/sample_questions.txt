# Sample Questions for Compliance Assessment

## Data Privacy and Security

1. What is the company's data retention policy for customer information?

2. How long are customer data backups retained?

3. What encryption standards are used for data at rest?

4. What encryption protocols are used for data in transit?

5. How often are security audits conducted?

6. Who is responsible for approving security exceptions?

7. What multi-factor authentication requirements are in place?

8. How frequently are user access rights reviewed?

## Compliance and Regulatory

9. Which regulations does the company comply with?

10. When was the last external audit conducted?

11. What is the timeline for addressing critical audit findings?

12. Is the company ISO 27001 certified?

13. What is the GDPR compliance status?

14. How are data subject rights requests handled?

## Infrastructure and Operations

15. What cloud provider hosts the production environment?

16. What is the Recovery Time Objective (RTO) for critical systems?

17. What is the Recovery Point Objective (RPO)?

18. How often are disaster recovery tests performed?

19. What is the current system uptime percentage?

20. What database technology is used in production?

## Incident Response

21. What is the mean time to detect (MTTD) security incidents?

22. What is the mean time to respond (MTTR) to incidents?

23. How many critical security incidents occurred in the last quarter?

24. What are the steps in the security incident response procedure?

## Network and Access Control

25. How is network segmentation implemented?

26. What firewall rules review frequency is maintained?

27. How many privileged accounts are currently active?

28. What is the completion rate for security awareness training?

## Backup and Recovery

29. What is the frequency of full database backups?

30. Where are backup copies stored for disaster recovery purposes?
